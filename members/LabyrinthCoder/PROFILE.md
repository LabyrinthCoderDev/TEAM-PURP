## About

Independent developer and researcher. Still learning. Building in public.

The question driving the work: *what if the structure between AI cognition and the world enforced accountability — not by training, not by prompting, not by hope — but by geometry?*

Labyrinth-OS is the answer being built toward. Not finished. Honest about what it is.

---

## Philosophy

> *"Imagination is free. Execution requires proof. No exception."*

The gate is not there to stop bad things.
The gate is there so good things can happen safely.

Lane 1 is free — imagination, speculation, generation — unconstrained.
Lane 2 is governed — execution passes through the gate, gets logged, can be replayed.

One crossing point. No bypass.
